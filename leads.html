Each contingent is required to have a contingent lead, who will direct the assembly and general forward movement of the contingent, and wheel monitor(s) any vehicles and/or floats.

Leads should identify themselves to their fellow contingent participants on the morning of the parade.

Contingent Lead Responsibilities

  1. Attend a parade briefing in advance of the parade, date to be determined.
  2. Wear the Contingent Lead identification sticker.
  3. Receive and distribute "wheel monitor" stickers to the appropriate people.

Responsibilities on Parade Day

  1. Receive the order placard and affix it to the parade contingent banner.
  2. Once the contingent is in place and ready, check-in using the button below.

Parade Day Assembly Area Check-In

On the morning of the parade, once your contingent is assembled and ready to go, please click this button to send a check-in message to the parade team. Click the button, enter your contingent number and hit send!
