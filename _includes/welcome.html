Oakland Pride™ is a time for the LGBTQ+ community to come together and celebrate its diversity, strength, and resilience. It is also a time to raise awareness of LGBTQ+ issues and to fight for equality.

The parade is significant for the city of Oakland and the LGBTQ+ rights movement nationally. It is a visible reminder of the presence and power of the LGBTQ+ community. It is also a powerful symbol of hope and progress.

By participating in the parade, you are making a statement about your support for the LGBTQ+ community. You are also helping to create a more inclusive and accepting world.

It will be a day to remember!

This is the 10th annual Oakland Pride Parade!
The first was held in 2014, the year of Oakland Pride's 5th Celebration.
