Parade Registration

Thank you for your interest in participating in the 2024 Parade. Please review the following information before proceeding with registration.

Participation Requirements

Before registering please ensure that your contingent is able to comply with the following requirements:

  1. All contingent participants must review and agree to the Oakland Pride Parade Participation Waiver.
  2. Contingents must have a minimum of 25 participants, and may have up to 500.
  3. Contingents must have a designated contingent leader who will:
    • be the primary contact for the contingent.
    • be responsible for keeping the continuous forward movement of the contingent along the parade route.
  4. Contingents with vehicles must
    • have designated wheel monitors to walk along either side of all vehicles.
    • show proof of vehicle registration, insurance, and valid driver's license.
  5. Contingents must have a compliant banner to display at the front. Banners can be ordered as part of the registration process.
